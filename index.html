What we do

Mission

To offer every student in IST the opportunity to learn skills needed for the future at no cost whatsoever.

Vision

To give students the skills and conviction to learn about technology and the possibilities that lie beyond it.

Tools we will use

We wil use the freecodecamp specialized curriculum, coupled with some practial exercises to achieve mastery in the field of web development.

Who we are

IST students who want to teach to others

We're students with backgrounds in web devlopment and we wish to teach others and integrate them in a community that has a passion for cooperation and collaboration.

Our course consists of learning HTML and CSS through lectures and practical projects, we will use tools that are readily available and easy to use. Don't worry if you are not able to code just yet, the program is designed so that anyone can code! Even if they don't have a background in coding.
